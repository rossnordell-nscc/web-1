HE/HIM

I am currently enrolled in Graphic Design at NSCC's Ivany Campus. I finally feel like I am doing what I am meant to be doing but it wasn't a straightforward journey to get here. I have been in Halifax for about 6 years and for most of that time I felt like I was just floating through my life. On my birthday two years ago I took a moment to reflect on what was important to me and I realized a lot of my favourite pastimes were creative. This lead me to NSCC's website which lead me to their AMCA program. The class that stood out the most that year was Graphic Design so I put a portfolio together and applied... now here I am!
